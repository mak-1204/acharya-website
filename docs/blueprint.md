# **App Name**: Acharya Ascent

## Core Features:

- Modern Multi-Page UI: Implementation of all specified 7 pages (Home, About, Courses, Results, Gallery, Scholarship, Contact) with a consistent, responsive, and performance-oriented user interface inspired by leading educational platforms.
- Dynamic Course Management: Dynamic sections for course segmentation, detailed course pages, and filterable course cards to effectively showcase offerings across various programs (JEE, NEET, CLAT, etc.).
- Lead Capture & Management Forms: Integration of user-friendly enquiry forms and scholarship interest forms across various pages, with direct data storage into distinct Firestore collections.
- Achievements & Credibility Showcase: Animated counters, auto-sliding carousels for student stars and testimonials, and dedicated results/awards sections to build trust and display success stories.
- Interactive FAQ Chatbot Tool: A floating chatbot widget providing instant, pre-defined answers to frequently asked questions about courses, fees, and admission processes, serving as a first point of contact through a rule-based AI tool.
- Global Navigation & Communication: Sticky and responsive navigation bar, prominent announcement bars, and floating utility buttons (WhatsApp, Call Now) for accessible information and direct communication pathways across the site.
- Firestore Data Persistence: Secure persistence of all form submission data (enquiries and scholarship_leads collections) into Firebase Firestore with appropriate schema and server timestamps, ensuring data integrity for lead management.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Red: '#D32F2F' for dominant call-to-action buttons, active navigation states, and main headings, conveying urgency and excitement, with white text for contrast.
- Primary Blue: '#1A237E' for secondary actions, key iconography, and subtle background elements, to instill trust and professionalism, also with white text for contrast.
- Background Color: '#F5F5F5' providing a light, clean, and neutral canvas for all content sections.
- Text Color: Dark charcoal '#1C1C1C' for primary content and body text, ensuring high readability.
- Highlight Color: Pure white '#FFFFFF' used for text on dark backgrounds and as a clean separator, and 'Success Green': '#2E7D32' for positive feedback and notifications.
- Body and Headline Font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) weights 400, 600, 700, providing a modern, precise, and contemporary feel suitable for both headings and body text.
- Consistent use of educational, success-oriented, and communication-related icons, primarily in the brand's primary red and blue, to visually reinforce content.
- Mobile-first responsive design, incorporating modern cards with 'rounded-xl' corners and 'shadow-md', varied section layouts including full-width banners, two-column structures, and masonry grids.
- Subtle and engaging animations including Framer Motion 'fade-in-up' on scroll for sections, 'count-up' effects for statistics, and auto-sliding carousels with 'pause on hover' and dot navigation.
